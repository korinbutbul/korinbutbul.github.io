Research Focus: DNP & NMR

My research focuses on the structural analysis of complex biological systems. I specialize in Nuclear Magnetic Resonance (NMR) spectroscopy, utilizing Dynamic Nuclear Polarization (DNP) to enhance sensitivity and unlock new possibilities in the study of DNA molecules.

By applying advanced DNP-enhanced NMR techniques, we aim to investigate the structural dynamics and folding of DNA at high resolution, providing insights into their functional roles in biological processes.
